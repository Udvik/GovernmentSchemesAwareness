Pradhan Mantri Vidya Lakshmi Karyakram

Launched by Ministry of Education on 15th August 2015

Eligibility:

1.Students who have secured admission into recognized courses in India or abroad are eligible to apply for educational loans through PMVLK.

2.The applicant must be an Indian citizen.

3.Students seeking loans for courses in various streams, including engineering, medical, management, post-graduation, etc., are eligible.

Benefits:

1.Wide Network of Banks: The platform includes a wide network of banks and financial institutions offering educational loans, increasing the options available to students.

2.Loan Tracking: Students can track the status of their loan applications through PMVLK, ensuring timely updates on the loan approval process.

3. Encouraging Higher Education: By simplifying the process of availing educational loans, PMVLK encourages students to pursue higher education and professional courses without financial constraints.
