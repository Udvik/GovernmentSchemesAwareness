Sarva Shiksha Abhiyaan

Launched by Government of India in 2000-2001

Eligibility:SSA is aimed at providing free and compulsory education to all children in the age group of 6 to 14 years, irrespective of their social or economic background. All children falling within this age range are eligible to avail the benefits of SSA.

Benefits:

1. Universal Elementary Education: SSA aims to achieve universal elementary education by ensuring that all children between 6 and 14 years have access to quality education.

2.Quality Education: SSA emphasizes enhancing the quality of education by providing adequate teaching-learning materials, training teachers, and implementing innovative teaching methodologies.

3. Textbooks and Learning Materials: The scheme provides free textbooks and learning materials to students to encourage regular attendance and active participation in education.
