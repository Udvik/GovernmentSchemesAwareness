RythuBima Scheme

Initiated by Government of Telangana in 2018

Eligibility: All farmers in Telangana state are eligible for Rythu Bima Scheme.

Benefits:

1. Crop Insurance Coverage: The scheme offers crop insurance coverage to farmers, protecting them from financial losses caused by crop damage due to natural calamities like drought, floods, cyclones, pests, diseases, etc.

2.Preventing Indebtedness: Crop failures can lead to a cycle of debt for farmers who rely on loans to fund their agricultural activities. The Rythu Bima Scheme helps prevent farmers from falling into debt traps by providing timely financial assistance in times of need.

3.Compensation for Crop Loss: In the event of crop damage or failure, eligible farmers receive compensation as per the insurance coverage, helping them recover the financial losses incurred during the crop cycle.
