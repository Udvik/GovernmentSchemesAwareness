Welcome to my Website

This website aims to provide information about various government schemes available for citizens


Rythu Bandhu Scheme

Under this scheme farmers receive financial assistance of Rs.10000 per acre per year to support the purchase of inputs for crop cultivation

Explore
Rythu Bima Scheme

A crop insurance scheme that provides coverage against crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases. It offers financial compensation to farmers in case of crop failure.

Explore
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana(PMFBY)

A crop insurance scheme that provides financial support to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Explore
Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

This scheme aims at enhancing the productivity of agriculture by promoting efficient water usage through various irrigation methods, including micro-irrigation techniques.

Explore
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

A credit scheme that provides farmers with timely and affordable credit for their agricultural and allied activities.

Explore
Mission Kakatiya

A program focused on the restoration of minor irrigation tanks and water bodies in rural areas. It aims to enhance water availability for agricultural purposes

Explore
Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA)

A flagship program aimed at universalizing elementary education by providing free and compulsory education to all children aged 6-14 years.

Explore
Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan(RMSA)

A scheme focused on improving access to secondary education and enhancing the quality of education in government schools.

Explore
Pradhan Mantri Vidya Lakshmi Karyakram

This initiative provides a single-window platform for students to access information and apply for various education loans, scholarships, and grants.

Explore
National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme(NMMSS)

A scholarship program that identifies talented students from economically weaker sections and supports their education by providing financial assistance.
