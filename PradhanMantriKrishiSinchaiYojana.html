Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Launched by Government of India on 1st July 2015

Eligibility: PMKSY is open to all farmers, including individual farmers, groups of farmers, and farmer cooperatives. The scheme is implemented across all states and union territories of India.

Benefits:

1. Sustainable Agriculture: PMKSY focuses on promoting sustainable agriculture practices by encouraging the adoption of modern irrigation technologies and water management practices.

2.Water Saving: PMKSY promotes water-saving irrigation techniques such as drip irrigation, sprinkler irrigation, and precision irrigation methods. These techniques help optimize water usage, reduce water wastage, and increase water use efficiency.

3.Improving Livelihoods: Increased agricultural productivity due to better irrigation facilities can lead to improved incomes and livelihoods for farmers.
