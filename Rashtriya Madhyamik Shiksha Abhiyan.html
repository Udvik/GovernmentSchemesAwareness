Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan

Launched by Government of India in 2009-2010

Eligibility:RMSA is primarily aimed at improving secondary education, and as such, it benefits students studying in government and government-aided secondary schools.

Benefits:

1. Infrastructure Development: RMSA aims to improve and strengthen the infrastructure of secondary schools by providing amenities such as classrooms, libraries, laboratories, computer labs, and sports facilities.

2.Student Scholarships: The scheme provides scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students to encourage them to continue their studies.

3. Quality Improvement: RMSA undertakes various measures to improve the overall quality of secondary education and to bring it up to par with international standards.
