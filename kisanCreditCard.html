Kisan Credit Card

Launched by Government of India in 1998

Eligibility: The KCC scheme is available to all farmers, including individual farmers, joint cultivators, and tenant farmers. The eligibility criteria may vary slightly from one financial institution to another, but generally, all farmers engaged in crop cultivation are eligible to apply.

Benefits:

1. Access to Timely Credit: KCC provides farmers with access to timely and hassle-free credit for their agricultural needs, such as the purchase of seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and other inputs required during the crop cycle.

2.Multiple Withdrawals: Farmers can make withdrawals from the credit limit multiple times, eliminating the need to apply for a new loan for each agricultural operation.

3. Affordable Interest Rates: KCC loans typically come with low-interest rates, making it cost-effective for farmers to avail credit for their agricultural activities.
