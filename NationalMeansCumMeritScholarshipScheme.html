National Means Cum Merit Scholarship Scheme

Launched by Ministry of Education in 2008

Eligibility:

1.The student must be studying in Class VIII in a recognized government, local body, or government-aided school.

2.The annual family income of the student should not exceed a specific limit, which may vary from state to state and year to year.

3.The candidate should have scored at least 55% marks or equivalent grade in Class VII examination.

Benefits:

1.Financial Support: NMMSS provides financial support to deserving students from economically weaker sections to continue their education at the secondary stage (Classes IX to XII).

2.Merit-based Scholarships: The scheme provides scholarships to students based on their merit, as determined by their academic performance.

3. Encouraging Meritorious Students: NMMSS encourages meritorious students to excel in their studies by recognizing and rewarding their academic achievements.
