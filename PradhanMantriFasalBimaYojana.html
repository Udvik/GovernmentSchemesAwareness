Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Launched by Government of India on 2016

Eligibility:

1. All farmers, including sharecroppers and tenant farmers, are eligible to apply for PMFBY.

2.The scheme is available to farmers across the country, covering all states and union territories.

3. Farmers should be involved in crop cultivation on the insured land to be eligible for coverage.

Benefits:

1. Financial Protection: PMFBY offers financial protection to farmers by providing insurance coverage against crop loss due to natural calamities (such as drought, flood, cyclone, hailstorm, etc.), pests, and diseases.

2.Encouraging Investment in Agriculture: With the assurance of financial protection, farmers are encouraged to make better investments in agricultural inputs, leading to improved crop quality and yield.

3.Premium Subsidy: The premium rates for crop insurance are subsidized by the government, making it more affordable for farmers to avail insurance coverage.
