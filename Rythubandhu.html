Rythu Bandhu Scheme

Initiated by Government of Telangana in 2018

Eligibility: All farmers holding cultivable land are eligible for Rythu Bandhu Scheme

Benefits:

1. Financial Assistance: Under the Rythu Bandhu Scheme, eligible farmers receive financial assistance in the form of direct income support. The government provides a fixed amount per acre of cultivated land to the farmers.

2.Income support: The scheme aims to provide a regular and predictable income to farmers, which helps them meet their agricultural expenses, including the purchase of seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and other agricultural inputs.

3.Incentive for farming: By providing income support per acre of cultivated land, the scheme incentivizes farmers to continue agricultural activities and encourages them to utilize the available land for cultivation.
