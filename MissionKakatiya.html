Mission Kakatiya

Initiated by Government of Telangana in 2018

Benefits:

1. Improved Water Storage: The restoration and desilting of minor irrigation tanks increase their water storage capacity. This leads to better availability of water for agricultural purposes throughout the year, even during dry spells.

2.Increased Irrigation Potential: Increased Irrigation Potential: With the revival of tanks and water bodies, the command area for irrigation expands, allowing farmers to cultivate larger areas of land and grow multiple crops in a year.

3.Increased Agricultural Productivity: The availability of water for irrigation positively impacts crop yields and agricultural productivity, leading to improved income and socio-economic development.
